Swasthya — AI-Powered Healthcare

Your Health Companion — A prototype AI healthcare web app designed for rural and semi-urban India.


About:

Swasthya is a frontend prototype of an AI-powered healthcare companion app built to address three core problems in India's healthcare system:

Misinformation — Millions rely on unsafe online health advice

Access gaps — Rural users can't easily reach qualified medical guidance

Self-medication risks — Delayed treatment from unguided self-diagnosis


Features:

🩺 Swasthya AI (Bot) - Chat-based symptom checker in English & Hindi

💊 Medicine Reminder - Add and toggle daily medicine reminders

🧾 Medicine Scanner - Simulated prescription scan & decode

🛒 Order Medicine - Browse and add medicines to cart

💡 Health Tips - Categorised daily wellness tips

🏥 Find Hospital - Nearby clinics and hospitals with contact info

📦 Order Tracker - View order history and delivery status

💬 Wellness Coach - Scripted AI wellness conversation

🍎 Diet Planner - Goal-based Indian meal plans with calorie tracker

😴 Sleep Tracker - Log and track sleep quality over time

🧠 Mental Health - Mood tracker, resources & breathing exercise

✏️ Edit Profile - Personal health profile with blood group, allergies

📋 Health Reports - View and download health reports


Tech Stack:

HTML5 / CSS3 / Vanilla JavaScript — No frameworks

Google Fonts (Poppins) — Only external dependency

Single file — Everything in one .html file


Demo Flow:

Open Swasthya.html

Enter your name and login

Try Swasthya AI — describe symptoms in English or Hindi

Explore Diet Planner, Sleep Tracker, Mental Health pages

Add a Medicine Reminder or browse the Order Medicine store
